# University Curriculum — Department Roadmaps

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-04

---

## Overview

Each department has its own 4-year curriculum (Year 100 → 400, 2 semesters each, 5 courses per semester). Every course includes:

- **Description:** What the student will learn and why it matters
- **Classes:** 5-12 classes with real YouTube URLs
- **AI Summary:** Key takeaways after completing the course
- **Related Articles:** 3 real articles from authoritative sources
- **Quiz:** 5 questions with answers

## Curriculum Structure

```
Department (e.g. Product Management)
  └── Year 100 (Foundations)
        ├── Semester 1 (5 courses)
        │     ├── PM101 — Foundations of Product Management
        │     │     ├── 12 classes with YouTube URLs
        │     │     ├── AI Summary
        │     │     ├── 3 Related Articles
        │     │     └── 5 Quiz Questions
        │     ├── DES101 — Introduction to Visual Design
        │     ├── PM103 — Introduction to Agile & Scrum
        │     ├── UXR102 — Fundamentals of User Research
        │     └── COM101 — Communication & Stakeholder Management
        └── Semester 2 (5 courses)
              ├── PM102 — Writing User Stories & Requirements
              ├── DES104 — Introduction to UX Design
              ├── ANA102 — Fundamentals of Product Analytics
              ├── DEV102 — HTML & CSS for Product Managers
              └── MKT102 — Fundamentals of Product Marketing
  └── Year 200 (Intermediate Craft)
  └── Year 300 (Advanced Systems & Strategy)
  └── Year 400 (Professional Practice & Leadership)
        └── Semester 2 always includes CAP402 — Capstone Project
```

## Class Structure

Each course has 5-12 classes (lessons). Each class includes:

- **Title**: Topic name (e.g. "What is a Product Manager?")
- **YouTube Resource**: Real, publicly available video link
- **Optional Timestamp**: Specific section of a longer video

---

## Course Structure

Each course includes:

- **Code & Title**: e.g. `PM101 — Foundations of Product Management`
- **Credits**: 3 credits (6 for Capstone)
- **Description**: 2-3 sentence overview
- **Classes**: 5-12 classes with YouTube URLs
- **AI Summary**: 2-3 sentence key takeaway
- **Related Articles**: 3 real articles from authoritative sources
- **Quiz**: 5 questions with answers
