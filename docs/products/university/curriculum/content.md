**Goal:** Produce graduates who can create engaging, authentic, and effective user-generated content for brands — from concept to published content.

*Related Articles:*

*Quiz (5 questions):*
1. What is UGC? — **Answer:** User-Generated Content — content created by real users/customers about a brand, not by the brand itself
2. What is the creator economy? — **Answer:** The ecosystem of content creators, platforms, tools, and brands that monetize content creation
3. What are the main types of content? — **Answer:** Video, photo, text, audio (podcasts), and live streams
4. Why is UGC more trusted than brand content? — **Answer:** It's perceived as authentic, unbiased, and created by real people with real experiences
5. What makes content creation a viable career in Nigeria? — **Answer:** Growing brand demand, social media expansion, affordable tools, and increasing monetization opportunities

1. What is the most important factor in smartphone photography? — **Answer:** Lighting — natural light is best, avoid harsh shadows and overexposure
2. What is the rule of thirds? — **Answer:** Dividing the frame into a 3x3 grid and placing the subject along the lines or intersections for balanced composition
