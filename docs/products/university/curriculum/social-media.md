**Goal:** Produce graduates who understand social media platforms, content creation, community management, and the fundamentals of social media strategy for Nigerian and global brands.

*Quiz (5 questions):*
1. What does a social media manager do? — **Answer:** Creates content, schedules posts, manages community, analyzes performance, and develops social strategy
2. What are the most important social platforms in Nigeria? — **Answer:** Instagram, Twitter (X), TikTok, WhatsApp, Facebook, and LinkedIn
3. What is the difference between social media marketing and social media management? — **Answer:** Marketing focuses on campaigns and paid ads; management covers the full spectrum — content, community, strategy, analytics, and customer service
4. Why is social media the dominant marketing channel in Nigeria? — **Answer:** High mobile penetration, young population, affordable data, and cultural preference for social-first communication
5. What is social media ethics? — **Answer:** Responsible practices around transparency, authenticity, privacy, and avoiding manipulation or harmful content

*Quiz (5 questions):*
1. What are the most important social media metrics? — **Answer:** Engagement rate, reach, impressions, follower growth, click-through rate, and conversion rate
2. What is social listening? — **Answer:** Monitoring social media for mentions of your brand, competitors, and industry — to understand sentiment and trends
3. What is the difference between reach and impressions? — **Answer:** Reach: unique people who saw your content; impressions: total times your content was displayed (including repeat views)
4. What should a social media report include? — **Answer:** Key metrics, trends, top-performing content, insights, and recommendations for improvement
5. How do you use analytics to optimize content? — **Answer:** Identify top-performing content types, analyze posting times, understand audience preferences, and double down on what works
